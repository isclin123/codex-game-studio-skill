# Monetization

Use for ads, IAP, premium, DLC, subscriptions, cosmetics, battle passes, energy systems, and ethical economy design.

## Models

- Premium: simple, player-friendly, good for PC/Steam and polished mobile niches.
- Ads: rewarded ads are usually better received than forced interstitials.
- IAP: cosmetics, convenience, content packs, currency; needs receipt validation.
- DLC: strong for PC/console content expansions.
- Subscription/pass: needs sustained content cadence.

## Mobile Checklist

- Define ad placements, frequency caps, and fail states.
- Implement consent/privacy flows where required.
- Restore purchases on iOS.
- Validate receipts server-side for durable goods and currency.
- Avoid blocking core usability behind confusing paywalls.

## Economy Health

- Track sources and sinks.
- Prevent runaway inflation.
- Model first-session earnings and first purchase opportunity.
- Keep progression fair and understandable.

## Ethics

- Avoid dark patterns, fake scarcity, hidden odds, and manipulative timers.
- Be extra careful with children or child-attractive games.
- Disclose probabilities for loot-style mechanics where required.
